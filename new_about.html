My Design Philosophy

I believe to create an emotional experience through a product, you must be driven by your own emotional experiences.

After I began my formal journey into digital design and computer programming, I realized that my love for design began way before I learned my way around any editor or IDE. Whether it be recreating unnecessarily specific life moments through a Spotify playlist, organizing lists of Letterbox movies that capture delightful cinematography, or spending hours organizing the perfect layout for a collage of posters on my dorm room wall, my eye for design helps me make sense of the world around me.

I believe there's comfort and purpose in the idea that we all have the power to be the designers of our own lives ‚Äî and I want to help people realize that through my work.
