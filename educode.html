EDUCODE

PRODUCT DESIGN

3.5 WEEKS

My goal in creating Educode was to make computer science education more widely accessible to kids who may not have access to a traditional computer science education or coding resources at their school. Through interactive lessons and challenges to help students learn and progress at their own pace, Educode seeks to provide an engaging platform that makes it easy for students to discover and pursue their interests in computer science.
